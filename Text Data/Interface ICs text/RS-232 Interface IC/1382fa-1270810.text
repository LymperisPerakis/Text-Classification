■   ESD Protection Over ±10kV                                                  saving 0.1µF capacitors.
■   Uses Small Capacitors: 0.1µF                                               The transceiver operates in one of two modes, Normal
■   Operates to 120kBaud                                                       and Shutdown. In the Normal mode, ICC is only 220µA
■   Output Overvoltage Does Not Force Current                                  with the driver outputs unloaded. In the Shutdown
    Back into Supplies                                                         mode, the charge pump is turned off, the driver outputs
■   RS232 I/O Lines Can Be Forced to ±25V                                      are forced into three-state, both receivers are off and
    Without Damage                                                             ICC drops to 0.2µA.
■   Pin Compatible with LT1180A

          U                                                                    overvoltage RS232 specifications. The transceiver can
