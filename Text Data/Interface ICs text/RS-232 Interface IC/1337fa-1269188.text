■   Low Supply Current: 300µA                                                  The LTC®1337 is a 3-driver/5-receiver RS232 transceiver
■   ESD Protection: Over ±10kV                                                 with very low supply current. In the no load condition, the
■   1µA Supply Current in Shutdown                                             supply current is only 300µA. The charge pump only
■   Operates from a Single 5V Supply                                           requires four 0.1µF capacitors and can supply up to 12mA
■   Uses Small Capacitors: 0.1µF                                               of extra current to power external circuitry.
■   Operates to 120k Baud                                                      In Shutdown mode, the supply current is further reduced
■   Three-State Outputs Are High Impedance When Off                            to 1µA. All RS232 outputs assume a high impedance state
■   Output Overvoltage Does Not Force Current Back into                        in Shutdown and with the power off.
    Supplies
■   RS232 I/O Lines Can Be Forced to ±25V without                              The LTC1337 is fully compliant with all data rate and
    Damage                                                                     overvoltage RS232 specifications. The transceiver can
■   Pin Compatible with LT ®1137A and LT1237                                   operate up to 120k baud with a 1000pF// 3kΩ load. Both
■   Flowthrough Architecture                                                   driver outputs and receiver inputs can be forced to ±25V
                                                                               without damage, and can survive multiple ±10kV ESD
