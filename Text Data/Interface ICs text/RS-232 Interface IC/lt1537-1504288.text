■    Operates from a Single 5V Supply                                                           devices. An advanced driver output stage operates up to
■    Easy PC Layout: Flow-through Architecture                                                  250kbaud while driving heavy capacitive loads.
■    Rugged Bipolar Design
■    Outputs Assume a High Impedance State When Off                                             The LT1537 is fully compliant with all RS232 specifica-
     or Powered Down                                                                            tions. Special bipolar construction techniques protect the
■    Improved Protection: RS232 I/O Lines Can Be Forced                                         drivers and receivers beyond the fault conditions stipu-
     to ±25V Without Damage                                                                     lated for RS232. Driver outputs and receiver inputs can be
