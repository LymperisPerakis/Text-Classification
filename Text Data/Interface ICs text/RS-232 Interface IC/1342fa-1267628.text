■    Pin Compatible with LT1137A                                                           All receivers and drivers assume high impedance states
■    120kBaud Operation for RL = 3k, CL = 2500pF                                           during shutdown.

■    CMOS Comparable Low Power: 60mW                                                       operating mode. When driver disable is high the charge
■    Operates from a 5V Supply and 3V Logic Supply                                         pump and drivers turn off. Receivers continue to operate
■    Easy PC Layout: Flowthrough Architecture                                              during driver disable.

     Off or Powered Down                                                                   survive multiple ±10kV strikes, eliminating the need for
