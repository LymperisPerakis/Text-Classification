                                                                                 Datasheet - production data
                                                       Description
                                                       The ST202EB, ST202EC, ST232EB, and
                                                       ST232EC are two-driver, two-receiver devices
                                                       designed for RS-232 and V.28 communications
                                                       in harsh environments. Each transmitter output
                                                       and receiver input is protected against ± 15 kV
                                                       electrostatic discharge (ESD) shocks. The drivers
                                                       meet all EIA/TIA-232E and CCITT V.28
                                                       specifications at data rates up to 230 kbps, when
                                                       loaded in accordance with the EIA/TIA-232E
                                                       specification. The ST202EB, ST202EC,
                                                       ST232EB, and ST232EC use a single 5 V supply
Features                                               voltage.
     ESD protection for RS-232 I/O pins: ± 15 kV
