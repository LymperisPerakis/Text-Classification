■   Four RS232 Transceivers or Two RS485                                          The LTC ®1334 is a low power CMOS bidirectional trans-
    Transceivers on One Chip                                                      ceiver featuring two reconfigurable interface ports. It can
■   Operates from a Single 5V Supply                                              be configured as two RS485 differential ports, as two dual
■   Withstands Repeated ±10kV ESD Pulses                                          RS232 single-ended ports or as one RS485 differential
■   Uses Small Charge Pump Capacitors: 0.1µF                                      port and one dual RS232 single-ended port. An onboard
■   Low Supply Current: 8mA Typical                                               charge pump requires four 0.1µF capacitors to generate
■   10µA Supply Current in Shutdown                                               boosted positive and negative supplies, allowing the RS232
■   Self-Testing Capability in Loopback Mode                                      drivers to meet the RS232 ±5V output swing requirement
■   Power-Up/Down Glitch-Free Outputs                                             with only a single 5V supply. A shutdown mode reduces
■   Driver Maintains High Impedance in Three-State,                               the ICC supply current to 10µA.
    Shutdown or with Power Off                                                    The RS232 transceivers are in full compliance with RS232
■   Thermal Shutdown Protection                                                   specifications. The RS485 transceivers are in full compli-
■   Receiver Inputs Can Withstand ±25V                                            ance with RS485 and RS422 specifications. All interface
          U                                                                       drivers feature short-circuit and thermal shutdown pro-
APPLICATIO S                                                                      tection. An enable pin allows RS485 driver outputs to be
                                                                                  forced into high impedance, which is maintained even
■   Low Power RS485/RS422/RS232/EIA562 Interface                                  when the outputs are forced beyond supply rails or power
■   Software-Selectable Multiprotocol Interface Port                              is off. Both driver outputs and receiver inputs feature
■   Cable Repeaters                                                               ±10kV ESD protection. A loopback mode allows the driver
■   Level Translators                                                             outputs to be connected back to the receiver inputs for
                                                                                  diagnostic self-test.
