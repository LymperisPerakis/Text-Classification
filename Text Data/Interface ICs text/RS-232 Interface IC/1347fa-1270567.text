FEATURES                                                                      DESCRIPTIO                                       U
■   Low Supply Current: 300µA                                                 The LTC®1347 is a 3-driver/5-receiver RS232 transceiver
■   Five Receivers Kept Alive in Shutdown                                     with very low supply current. In the no load condition, the
■   ESD Protection Over ±10kV                                                 supply current is only 300µA. The charge pump only
■   Operates from a Single 5V Supply                                          requires four 0.1µF capacitors.
■   Uses Small Capacitors: 0.1µF                                              In Shutdown mode, all five receivers are kept alive and the
■   Operates to 120kBaud
                                                                              supply current is 80µA. All RS232 outputs assume a high
■   Three-State Outputs Are High Impedance When Off                           impedance state in Shutdown and with the power off.
■   Output Overvoltage Does Not Force Current Back
    into Supplies                                                             The LTC1347 is fully compliant with all data rate and
■   RS232 I/O Lines Can Be Forced to ±25V                                     overvoltage RS232 specifications. The transceiver can
    Without Damage                                                            operate up to 120kbaud with a 2500pF, 3kΩ load. Both
■   Pin Compatible with LT1137A and LT1237                                    driver outputs and receiver inputs can be forced to ±25V
■   Flowthrough Architecture                                                  without damage, and can survive multiple ±10kV ESD
          U                                                                   strikes.
