The ISL32430E, ISL32432E, ISL32433E, ISL32435E, and
ISL32437E are 3.3V to 5V powered, fault protected, extended
                                                                        Features
common-mode range differential transceivers for balanced                • Fault protected RS-485 bus pins . . . . . . . . . . . . . . up to ±40V
communication. The RS-485 bus pins, driver outputs and                  • Extended common-mode range. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . ±15V
receiver inputs, are protected against overvoltages up to ±40V            larger than required for RS-485
and against ±15kV ESD strikes. Additionally, these transceivers
                                                                        • ±15kV HBM ESD protection on RS-485 bus pins
operate in environments with common-mode voltages up to
±15V (exceeds the RS-485 requirement), making this RS-485               • Wide supply range . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 3V to 5.5V
family one of the most robust on the market.                            • Cable invert pin corrects for reversed cable connections
The transmitters are RS-485 compliant with VCC ≥ 4.5V and                 while maintaining Rx full fail-safe functionality (ISL32437E
deliver a 1.1V differential output voltage into the RS-485                only)
specified 54Ω load even with VCC = 3V.                                  • 1/4 Unit load for up to 128 devices on the bus
The receiver (Rx) inputs feature a full fail-safe design that           • High transient overvoltage tolerance. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . ±60V
ensures a logic high Rx output if the Rx inputs are floating,           • Full fail-safe (open, short, terminated) RS-485 receivers
shorted, or on a terminated but undriven (idle) bus. The Rx full
fail-safe operation is maintained even when the Rx input polarity       • Choice of RS-485 data rates . . . . . . . . . . . . . 250kbps or 1Mbps
is switched (cable invert function on ISL32437E).                       • Low quiescent supply current. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 2.1mA
The ISL32437E includes a cable invert function that reverses            • Ultra low shutdown supply current. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 10µA
the polarity of the Rx and Tx bus pins if the cable is                  • Pb-free (RoHS compliant)
misconnected during installation.
