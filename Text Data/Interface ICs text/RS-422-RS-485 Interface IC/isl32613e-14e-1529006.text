Application Information                                                                           The ISL32613E and ISL32614E operate with VCC as low as 1.8V.
                                                                                                  When coupled with the ISL32610E or ISL32611E 1.8V receivers,
Driver Features                                                                                   they provide a differential communication link optimized for very
The ISL32613E and ISL32614E transmitters are differential                                         low power and for slow data rates. Figures 9 and 10 illustrate the
