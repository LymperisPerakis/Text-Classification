-7V to +12V. Receivers are specially designed to fail-safe to a       ■  Controlled driver slew rates
logic high output state if the inputs are left un-driven or               115kbps, Low EMI (SP3082E)
shorted. All RS-485 bus-pins are protected against severe                 500kbps, Low EMI (SP3083E, SP3084E, SP3085E)
