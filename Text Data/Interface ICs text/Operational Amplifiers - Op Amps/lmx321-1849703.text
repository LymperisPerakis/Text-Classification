          The LMX321, LMX358, and LMX324 can operate from 2.3 V to 5.5 V. The devicesâ€™

          The LMX321, LMX358, and LMX324 have an input common-mode range that includes
          ground. The input common-mode range is extended from VCC- - 0.2 V to VCC+ - 1 V, with no
          output phase reversal.
