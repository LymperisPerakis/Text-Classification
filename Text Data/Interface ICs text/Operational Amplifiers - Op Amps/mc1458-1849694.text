                                                      Description
                                                      The MC1458 is a high-performance, monolithic,
                                                      dual operational amplifier intended for a wide
Features                                              range of analog applications. The high gain and
     Low power consumption                            wide range of operating voltages provide superior
     Large input voltage range                        performance in integrator, summing amplifiers,
     No latch-up                                      and general feedback applications.

     Short-circuit protection
     No frequency compensation required
