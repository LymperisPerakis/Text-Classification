Description
Tripp Lite's 5-meter ( 16ft) multimode duplex fiber optic SC/ST patch cable is manufactured from 50/125 zip cord fiber. The cable has ST to SC connectors, a
PVC jacket and is FDDI and OFNR rated. Duplex multimode fiber is most commonly used in LAN applications.
Features
   Manufactured from 50/125 duplex (zip cord) fiber
   PVC jacket
   Length: 5-meters(16ft) - Connectors: 2 ST to 2 SC connectors
   Insertion loss testing performed on every connector (0.2db typical) and provided with cable
   Beveled edge on ends of glass makes insertion of plug a breeze
   Fiber made from glass (not a polymer)
   Fiber optic distributed data interface (FDDI) rated
   OFNR (riser rated)
