Description
Tripp Lite's 2-meter ( 6ft-7ft) multimode duplex fiber optic SC/ST patch cable is manufactured from 50/125
zipcord fiber. The cable has ST to SC connectors, a PVC jacket and is FDDI and OFNR rated. Duplex
multimode fiber is most commonly used in LAN applications.
Features
●  Manufactured from 50/125 duplex (zipcord) fiber
●  PVC jacket
●  Length: 2-meters(6ft-7ft) - Connectors: 2 ST to 2 SC connectors
●  Insertion loss testing performed on every connector (0.2db typical) and provided with cable
●  Beveled edge on ends of glass makes insertion of plug a breeze
●  Fiber made from glass (not a polymer)
●  Fiber optic distributed data interface (FDDI) rated
●  OFNR (riser rated)
