Description
                                                                                                          Fiber Patch Cable, LC/LC
Tripp Lite's 5-meter, single mode duplex fiber optic LC/LC patch cable is manufactured from 8.3/125
zipcord fiber. The cable has LC connectors on one end, LC connectors on the other, a PVC jacket, and is
FDDI and OFNR rated. Duplex single mode fiber is most commonly used in LAN applications.
Features
● Manufactured from 8.3/125 duplex (zipcord) fiber
● PVC jacket
● Length: 5-meter. Connectors: 2 LC and 2 LC connectors on each end
● Insertion loss testing performed on every connector (0.2db typical) and provided with cable
● Beveled edge on ends of glass makes insertion of plug a breeze
● Fiber made from glass (not a polymer)
● Fiber optic distributed data interface (FDDI) rated
● OFNR (riser rated)
