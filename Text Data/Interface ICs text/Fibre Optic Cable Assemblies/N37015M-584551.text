Description
Tripp Lite's 15-meter ( 50ft ), singlemode duplex fiber optic LC/LC patch cable is manufactured from 8.3/125 zipcord fiber. The cable has LC connectors on one
end, LC connectors on the other, a PVC jacket, and is FDDI and OFNR rated. Duplex singlemode fiber is most commonly used in LAN applications.
Features
   Manufactured from 8.3/125 duplex (zipcord) fiber
   PVC jacket
   Length: 15-meter ( 50ft ) Connectors: 2 LC and 2 LC connectors on each end
   Insertion loss testing performed on every connector (0.2db typical) and provided with cable
   Beveled edge on ends of glass makes insertion of plug a breeze
   Fiber made from glass (not a polymer)
   Fiber optic distributed data interface (FDDI) rated
   OFNR (riser rated)
