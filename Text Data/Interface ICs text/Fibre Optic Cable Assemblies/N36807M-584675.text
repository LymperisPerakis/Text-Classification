Description
                                                                                                        ● 7-meter Duplex Singlemode
Tripp Lite's 7-meter, singlemode duplex fiber optic LC/ST patch cable is manufactured from 8.3/125
                                                                                                          8.3/125 Fiber Patch Cable,
zipcord fiber. The cable has LC connectors on one end, ST connectors on the other, a PVC jacket, and is
FDDI and OFNR rated. Duplex singlemode fiber is most commonly used in LAN applications.                   LC/ST
Features
●  Manufactured from 8.3/125 duplex (zipcord) fiber
●  PVC jacket
●  Length: 7-meters/Connectors: 2 LC and 2 ST connectors on each end
●  Insertion loss testing performed on every connector (0.2db typical) and provided with cable
●  Beveled edge on ends of glass makes insertion of plug a breeze
●  Fiber made from glass (not a polymer)
●  Fiber optic distributed data interface (FDDI) rated
●  OFNR (riser rated)
