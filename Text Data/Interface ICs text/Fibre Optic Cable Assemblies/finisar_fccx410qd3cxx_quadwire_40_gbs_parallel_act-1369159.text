III.     Connectivity Diagnostics® Technology
Finisar’s FCCx410QD3Cxx Quadwire® AOCs are equipped with Connectivity
Diagnostics®, which combines out-of-band signaling between the transceivers at the two
ends of the AOC, and LED lights embedded in their pull tabs.
The Quadwire® implements two Connectivity Diagonostics® features:
                                 Helps locate the other end of the AOC. Manually pushing-in
                                 either pull tab will light up their LED lights on both ends.
                                 Reports the health status of the AOC. Any Digital Diagnostic
                                 Monitoring warning/alarm events, transmitter fault or receiver
                                 loss of signal will light up the pull-tab LED lights on both ends.
A detailed description of Connectivity Diagnostics® is provided in Finisar’s Application Note
AN-2158 [4].

IX.     Regulatory Compliance
Finisar Quadwire® active optical cables are RoHS-6 Compliant. Copies of certificates are
available at Finisar Corporation upon request.
Quadwire® active optical cables are Class 1 laser eye safety compliant per IEC 60825-1.
The round cable jacket is available in both plenum-rated (OFNP) and riser-rated (OFNR)
low smoke zero-halogen (LSZH).
